Vijay was born on June 22, 1974. His father, S. A. Chandrasekhar, is a film director, and his mother, Shoba Chandrasekhar, is a playback singer.
He debuted in the lead role in the early 1990s with Naalaiya Theerpu.
He has acted in many commercially successful films and is recognized for his dancing skills and singing talent.
Vijay is also known for his philanthropic activities and has been featured on the Forbes India Celebrity 100 list
